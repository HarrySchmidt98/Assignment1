Goals:

To sell the sport of Beach Volleyball to people of all ages and to promote it as a family sport. While also promoting the venues professional competition, their licensed bar with happy hour and the option to hire the venue for functions. And to also sell the venue as the fun family welcoming place that it is.

Success Evaluation: Success of the website will be measured by an increase in teams signing up for the competitive season and by increase of families and young adults attending the venue as well ans an increase in bookings for the venue.

 

Target Audience:

Target audience of the website is people aged from 15 - 30. This will be done by incorporating photos, videos and brighter colours with a simplistic design. This will make it easy to navigate and easy to understand.
