Slammers Beach Volleyball

There are around 800 million players worldwide who play volleyball at least once a week. Volleyball is a sport that is safe, fun but does not require extreme fitness. Since you don’t have to move around the court too much, it’s great for people who want a low-impact sport, that will help them get fit, without pushing them to their limit. For those who are a bit more competitive, the games are really exciting and intense and are great fun when competing against your family, friends or a challenging team in the competition season, but there’s always a net between you and your opponents.
Beach volleyball is suitable for people of all ages, and men and women alike
